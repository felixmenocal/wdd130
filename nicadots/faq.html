To answer this question, we need to review two concepts that are related and for that reason are often used interchangeably:
Web page: It is a document that can be displayed in a web browser (such as Google Chrome)
Website: It is a collection of web pages that are grouped together and are usually connected in various ways.

By creating a website, you are creating your online presence. This allows you to connect with people you might not otherwise be able to reach.

Choose and register a web domain name.
Choose a hosting or web hosting plan.
Choose a service provider of your choice for the design.
Provide/approve the content of your business that will be presented to your potential clients.
Do you have a logo? It is commonly found in the header of your business website
With all these elements together, you are ready to publish your website.

Visit our Rates page for more details.

The cost for the construction of your website is a single payment. However, the hosting of your website is a payment that is renewed annually. Depending on the type of website you can pay for the maintenance/update of your site as needed.

What are the main benefits?

  • It is the control center for all the marketing of your business.
  • It is the showcase to sell your products or services.
  • It helps you generate leads (and makes it easier to retain customers).
  • It allows you to compete with big brands.
  • It helps you build credibility.
  • A website for business is cheap (and very profitable).
