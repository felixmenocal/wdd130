FAQ

We love to answer your questions

What is a web page or website?
To answer this question, we need to review two concepts that are related and for that reason are often used interchangeably:
Web page: It is a document that can be displayed in a web browser (such as Google Chrome)
Website: It is a collection of web pages that are grouped together and are usually connected in various ways.
Why is it so important to have a website for your business?
By creating a website, you are creating your online presence. This allows you to connect with people you might not otherwise be able to reach.
What do I have to do to have a website?
Choose and register a web domain name. Choose a hosting or web hosting plan Choose a service provider of your choice for the design. Provide/approve the content of your business that will be presented to your potential clients. Do you have a logo? It is commonly found in the header of your business website
With all these elements together, you are ready to publish your website. How much does a web page cost me?
Visit our Rates page for more details. The cost of my website is a single payment or a monthly payment?
The cost for the construction of your Web site is a single payment. However, the hosting of your website is a payment that is renewed annually. Depending on the type of website you can pay for the maintenance/update of your site as needed.
